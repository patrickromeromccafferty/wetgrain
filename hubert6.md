## The boy <br>
<br>
They came to the village looking for him <br>
because there was no other way <br>
to get out and face the rivals. <br>
It became a habit to see vultures <br>
seeking their carrion in the outposts <br>
ransacking passengers’ ayates <br>
and rummaging through skirts, in search of flesh. <br>
<br>
He left his marbles behind, <br>
on the line with storm clouds of nostalgia, <br>
he left the multicolored fish <br>
with the dreams of the night <br>
and left among the coffee plantations <br>
the ropes on which misery swings. <br>
<br>
In his bones began to grow <br>
the cries of his friends, the girls of Marutsíí, <br>
who asked an ear of the dust and the stones <br>
so they wouldn’t take him away. <br>
<br>
They hooded him with scales of the afternoon <br>
and they hung an AK-47 on his shoulder <br>
and three deer-eye rosaries round his neck <br>
and he set about hunting men <br>
inviting death to his table. <br>
<br>
Since then <br>
they say that we, the people of the Mountain, <br>
are good at that <br>
and they keep coming to take our young boys away <br>
and sow death in their hands. <br>
<br>
<br>
<br>

## Adà <br>
<br>
Nìguwá gí’yáà ènè xàbò tsí jùdá ajwàn’ ná xuajñùún <br>
numuu rí nìgi’duu xkujndu <br>
khamí nangwá ì’gúùn mùtsañuú gajmíí i’wìí xàbò, <br>
tsetse ma’ nàwi’ñuú dxá’án tsú’kwè, <br>
ikhiin tsí nà’phò xuwi ná ndawaá xuajñàn ló’, <br>
ikhiin tsí nùrigú amaà’ ná ajwàn’ xkàradi, <br>
ikhiin tsí nùkuxè jnàwùún khamí nònè ngínií ijíín gò’ò ná júbàá. <br>
<br>
Xó ma’ ikhaa nìnìñuu xndú tsígun’ ná awùún rómbò <br>
ná ikhoo idi rí jàgù tsíngína, <br>
khamí nìnìñuu ìgì’ tsí mixtìín ná awùún xnú’ndoo mbro’on, <br>
khamí nìnìñuú ná awùún ixè kafé <br>
tsímbi ná nàxtráka mìnà’ rí tsíngína ló’. <br>
<br>
Xó ma’ ná awùún ìtsuu nì’kà ràgajàà iya idùún <br>
ijíín gò’ò Marutsíí tsí nimbáxuún gàjmìí,
ijíín gò’ò tsú’kwè nìndxá’wè ná nìtuxuù yujndà’ khamí ìtsí, <br>
rí màxá’gá jí’yáà gònè xàbò. <br>
<br>
Xàbò tsí judà ajwàn’ nìxùdaa xtá inuu, <br>
khamí nìstrákèè mbá xkamídá rí mbijwà ná xpaphòò, <br>
atsú tsákuun iduu àñà’ ná ñawúùn, <br>
ikànjgó ikhaa nìgí’dúù nìgudiín xàbò <br>
mí ni’gií rí tsíngína inuu ixè xaphoo xuajñúùn. <br>
<br>
Numuu mbi’i rú’kwè, nakhi rí nìgàji’yaà <br>
nuthèèn rí tsàá xàbò júbà ñajwàn xò’, <br>
maján ènè xò’ ñajun rú’kwè mé’ <br>
numuu rú’kwè tsetse nàguwá xàbò <br>
tsí nagòó ju’diín ijíín xuajen. <br>
khamí nudii ga’khò ná ñawùún èjèn tsú’kwè. <br>
<br>
<br>

*Hubert Matiúwàa (trans.. Juana Adcock)*
